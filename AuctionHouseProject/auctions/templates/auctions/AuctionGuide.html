Live Auction

AuctionHouse provides comprehensive services to sell all types of properties across the India.

Liked a property? Bid on your favorite one and get the best deal by following some easy steps-:

  • Register yourself with AuctionHouse
  • Select your desired location in which you want to purchase the property
  • Check out the properties listed and select your favorite one
  • Visit the property During Viewing Hours
  • Pay a nominal registration fee*
  • Bid the amount you agree to pay for the property in a given time frame
  • Time will be extended with 3 minutes if the bidding is done during last 3 minutes, Consider it as virtual hammer.
  • At the end of the auction timer the highest bidder will be considered as winner.
  • If you are the lucky one, then the property is yours!

Note:  No extra cost apart  from nominal registration fee based on actual property price will be charged from bidders
